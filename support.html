Cargo Logistics Explained

Our comprehensive cargo logistics services can handle all your shipping needs, from pick-up to delivery. We offer competitive rates and reliable transit times.
Understanding Freight vs. Cargo

While the terms "freight" and "cargo" are sometimes used interchangeably, there is a slight difference. Freight typically refers to goods transported by land (trucks, trains), while cargo can encompass goods shipped by air or sea as well.

What are the types of cargo you handle?

At KentexCargo, we handle a wide range of cargo types including but not limited to general goods, perishable items, oversized cargo, and hazardous materials.

How can I track my shipment?

You can easily track your shipment by using our online tracking system. Simply enter your tracking number on our website to get real-time updates on the status and location of your cargo.

What are your shipping rates?

Our shipping rates vary depending on factors such as the weight, dimensions, and destination of the cargo. Please contact our customer service team or check our website for a personalized quote.

How long does it take for a shipment to arrive?

The delivery time depends on various factors such as the shipping method, distance, and any customs clearance processes. We strive to deliver your cargo as quickly as possible while ensuring its safety and security.

Do you offer insurance for shipments?

Yes, we offer insurance options for shipments to provide you with peace of mind. Our insurance policies cover various risks such as loss, damage, or theft during transit. Please contact our customer service team for more details on insurance options.

Can I track my shipment online?

Yes, we provide online tracking services for all shipments. You can track the status and location of your cargo in real-time through our website or mobile app. Simply enter your tracking number to access the latest information about your shipment.
